Connect your Instagram with your Wordpress website allowing you to display your recent instagram photos to your wordpress site.

== Description ==

[DM Instagram](http://donmhi.co/projects/dm-instagram) allows you to share / display your latest photos on your wordpress website. You can display the images on your
posts and pages using a shortcode, or on any widget-ready area using the DM Instagram widget.

= Features: =

* Responsive-ready template
* Option to use Fotorama as the viewer
* Supports prettyPhoto
* Ability to display as a default gallery or with stunning slider with thumbnail navigation
* Allow touch and mouse drag thumbnail navigation
* Very easy to get it running
* Ability to choose transition animation
* Allow autoplay
* User-defined interval time
* Supports shortcode and widget to display anywhere on your wordpress website
